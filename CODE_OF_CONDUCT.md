## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all members of our community, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Values

We believe in:

- **Inclusivity**: Everyone's voice matters
- **Respect**: Treat everyone with dignity
- **Collaboration**: Great ideas come from working together
- **Learning**: We grow by helping each other
- **Safety**: A harassment-free environment for all

## Expected Behavior

All community members are expected to:

### Be Respectful

- Use inclusive language
- Welcome people of all backgrounds
- Be open to different perspectives
- Show empathy and kindness

### Be Helpful

- Offer constructive feedback
- Help others learn and grow
- Share knowledge generously
- Give credit where it's due

### Be Professional

- Keep discussions on-topic
- Avoid emotional reactions
- Focus on ideas, not personal attacks
- Respect intellectual property

### Be Honest

- Acknowledge mistakes gracefully
- Give proper credit
- Be truthful in communications
- Stand by your words

## Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate:

- **Harassment**: Any form of unwelcome conduct or communication
- **Discrimination**: Based on any protected characteristic
- **Hate Speech**: Slurs, offensive language, or hateful jokes
- **Personal Attacks**: Insults, mockery, or derogatory remarks
- **Trolling**: Deliberately provoking or disrupting discussion
- **Spam**: Unwanted promotional content or mass messaging
- **Doxxing**: Sharing private information without consent
- **Exclusion**: Deliberately excluding people from spaces
- **Retaliation**: Punishing someone for reporting a violation

## Reporting Guidelines

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document it**: Note what happened, when, who was involved
2. **Don't engage**: Don't respond to harassment or bad behavior
3. **Report it**: Contact us directly
4. **Follow up**: Provide any additional information requested

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this code of conduct may face:

- **Verbal Warning**: For minor first-time offenses
- **Written Warning**: For continued violations
- **Temporary Ban**: 7-30 day suspension from participation
- **Permanent Ban**: For severe or repeated violations

Decisions are made case-by-case with consideration for context and impact.
